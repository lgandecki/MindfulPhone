Quitting apps entirely?
You lose more than you gain.

You deleted Instagram and your mind got calmer. But you also lost touch with friends, missed events, and couldn't share your work when it mattered. The problem was never the apps — it was the autopilot. X is how you discover opportunities. YouTube is how you learn. Instagram is how you stay connected. You need them. You just don't need them all the time.

How it works

A conversation, not a countdown

1

Choose your apps

Pick the apps you find most distracting — social media, games, doomscrolling favorites. MindfulPhone puts a gentle shield over them.

2

Explain your reason

When you try to open a blocked app, Claude AI asks why. "I need to check a work message" or "I want to show someone my portfolio" — real reasons get you in.

3

Stay accountable

Claude remembers your patterns. Late night? Procrastinating? Third time today? It pushes back — not with rigidity, but with understanding.
